Instructions for Novena de Nossa Senhora Desatadora de Nós

A complete guide to fully experience the 7 days of prayer

Spiritual Preparation

Before beginning the 7-day lent, it's important to prepare yourself spiritually to receive the graces of this special period of prayer.

  • Confession: If possible, receive the sacrament of confession before starting the lent to begin with a purified heart.
  • Intention: Clearly define what special grace you wish to ask for during these 7 days. Write it on a piece of paper to keep it in mind.
  • Preparatory Fasting: If your health allows, consider a small fast the day before starting the lent (such as abstaining from meat, sweets, or social media).
  • Preliminary Study: Dedicate a few moments to learn more about Nossa Senhora Desatadora de Nós and his devotion to the guardian angel.

"Pray, have faith, and don't worry. Worry is useless. God is merciful and will hear your prayer." — Nossa Senhora Desatadora de Nós

Recommended Times

Choosing the ideal time for your daily prayers significantly contributes to the effectiveness of your lent. We recommend the following times:

Morning (5-7 AM)

The "Golden Hour" – upon waking, the mind is more receptive and less distracted by the day's concerns. NOSSA SENHORA began his prayers at 4 AM.

Night (9-11 PM)

Before sleeping, this is a time of silence and reflection, allowing for deeper prayer and a blessed sleep.

Angelic Hour (6 PM)

Traditionally the time to pray the Angelus, it is considered a special time for communication with angels.

Important Tip

Try to keep the same time every day to create a regular prayer habit. Set an alarm on your phone as a reminder and put it on "do not disturb" mode during your prayer time.

Prayer Environment

The environment where you pray directly influences the quality of your prayer. Create a space that invites contemplation:

  • Specific Location: Set aside a small corner in your home specifically for prayer. It doesn't need to be large, just a space dedicated exclusively to this purpose.
  • Eliminate Distractions: Turn off the TV, computer, and phone. Let family members know you need a few minutes of silence.
  • Adequate Lighting: Prefer natural light or candles instead of harsh lights. Dim lighting helps create a contemplative atmosphere.
  • Moderate Comfort: Use a comfortable chair or cushion, but avoid positions that might make you sleepy.
  • Sacred Symbols: Have a crucifix, an image of NOSSA SENHORA, and of your guardian angel in your prayer space.

"The place of prayer doesn't matter much; what matters is that we pray with total confidence, deep faith, and ardent love." — Nossa Senhora Desatadora de Nós

To fully experience this 7-day journey, we recommend having on hand:

Holy Bible

For the recommended readings on each day of the lent.

White or Yellow Candle

It symbolizes the divine light and angelic presence. Light it during your prayers.

Spiritual Journal

To write down inspirations, graces received, and experiences during the lent.

Rosary

NOSSA SENHORA always carried his rosary and called it "his weapon."

Image of Nossa Senhora Desatadora de Nós

To help with concentration and to invoke his intercession.

How to Pray the Prayers

For a deep and transformative prayer experience, follow these guidelines:

  • Sign of the Cross: Always begin with the sign of the cross, invoking the Holy Trinity.
  • Invocation to the Holy Spirit: Ask for the enlightenment of the Holy Spirit before beginning your prayer.
  • Slow Reading: Read each prayer slowly, savoring the words and allowing them to penetrate your heart.
  • Pauses for Silence: Take pauses at key moments in the prayer to internalize what you have just prayed.
  • Conclusion: Always end with an Our Father, Hail Mary, and Glory Be, thanking God, Our Lady, and Nossa Senhora Desatadora de Nós.
Our Lady's Method of Prayer

NOSSA SENHORA taught a simple and profound way to pray:

  1. External and internal silence (calming the body and mind)
  2. Confident dialogue with God as a friend
  3. Sincere humility (recognizing your dependence on God)
  4. Perseverance in times of dryness

Maximizing the Spiritual Benefits

To obtain the greatest fruits from this 7-day journey, consider these complementary practices:

  • Mass and Eucharist: If possible, attend daily Mass or at least on Sundays during the lent.
  • Charity: Perform one act of charity per day, dedicating it to your specific intention.
  • Complementary Readings: Read excerpts from the writings or biography of Nossa Senhora Desatadora de Nós to deepen your connection with him.
  • Prayer Partner: Find a friend or family member to pray with or to share your experiences.
  • Perseverance: Commit to completing the 7 days without interruption, even if you encounter difficulties.

"Prayer is the best weapon we have; it is the key that opens the heart of God." — Nossa Senhora Desatadora de Nós

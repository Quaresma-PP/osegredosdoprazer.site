DAY 6

Prayer of Gratitude

"Gratitude is a virtue that opens the gates of heaven; when we give thanks, even before seeing the graces realized, we demonstrate faith and humility."

— Inspired by the teachings of Nossa Senhora Desatadora de Nós

Reflection

On the sixth day of our journey, we dedicate ourselves to gratitude—a final prayer of praise and thanksgiving to God. After days of presenting supplications, requests, and longings, it is right and healthy to turn our gaze to all that the Lord has already done and continues to do in our lives, and to say from the heart: thank you so much, my God!

NOSSA SENHORA lived in constant thanksgiving, even in the midst of difficulties. He was aware that everything is grace and that we are never alone in our struggles.

Throughout these days, you have trusted in the help of your guardian angel, pleaded for healing, prosperity, protection, and peace. Regardless of whether you have already noticed visible changes or not, believe that God has heard every prayer and, at the opportune moment, will respond in the wisest and most loving way.

Full Prayer

"God of love and goodness, I reach the end of this journey with a heart overflowing with gratitude. How can I not thank You for all the graces You have granted me throughout this spiritual journey? Thank you, Lord, for being with me in every prayer, for hearing every plea—even those I could not express well in words, but which the Holy Spirit carried to You with ineffable groanings.

I thank You, Heavenly Father, for the gift of life, for my family, for my faithful Guardian Angel, for the faith You have sown in me, and for all the times You have supported me without my noticing. Thank you for the healings You have performed—whether visible or hidden. If my health has improved, I praise You joyfully; if I still await complete healing, I praise You also, for I trust that You are at work and giving me strength each day.

I thank You for the providence that has not allowed me to lack what is necessary; I recognize that everything good I have has come from Your generous hands. Thank you for the deliverances and protection granted to my family, often through the intercession of the Saints and Angels. Lord, thank you so much for the peace I feel now in my heart. Perhaps there are still battles to be fought, but today I realize that I am not alone—You walk with me, holding my hand. Blessed are You because You are faithful and keep Your promises!

Just as the Blessed Virgin Mary sang in her Magnificat, 'My soul magnifies the Lord, and my spirit rejoices in God my Savior,' I also want to magnify You with my simple words: Blessed are You, O God of my life, today and always!

Thank you, Jesus, for having inspired Nossa Senhora Desatadora de Nós to be an instrument in my spiritual life. I thank you for each of his teachings that touched me on this path: his words resonated in my heart, giving me the courage to pray and persevere. 'Pray, have faith, and don't worry'—I will carry this precious motto of NOSSA SENHORA with me, trusting ever more in You.

I also thank You, Lord, for giving me Mary as a loving mother—how many times I have felt her affection throughout these days! Thank you, little Mother of Heaven, for interceding for me and covering me with your mantle of protection. I thank the holy angels, especially my guardian angel, for their constant assistance. How many dangers avoided, how many inspirations received! I bless You, Father, for creating these beings of light to guard us.

In this moment of final gratitude, I surrender my entire being to You. All that I have and am comes from You and returns to You. If I have any talent, any fruitful work, if I have achieved any victory in these days, I attribute it all to Your grace. All honor and all glory be given to You, Lord! I also want to thank You for the future You are preparing for me. I do not know what will come, but I know it will be filled with Your presence. I praise You in advance for the blessings You will yet pour out and even for the crosses You will permit, for I believe that in all things You will be with me for my good.

Finally, I ask that You receive this humble thanksgiving of mine as a perfume of gratitude that rises to Your altar. May it unite with the eternal thanksgiving that Jesus, Your Son, offers in heaven for us, and thus be pleasing to You. Thank you so much, my God, for everything. I love You and trust in You. May I be a witness to Your wonders and always continue in prayer, not just for these days, but every day of my life. Amen."

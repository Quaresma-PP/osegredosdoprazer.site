DAY 2

Prayer for Healing

"Worry is useless. Our merciful Lord will hear your prayer."

— Nossa Senhora Desatadora de Nós

Reflection

This second day is dedicated to prayers for healing—be it the physical healing of bodily illnesses, or the inner healing of the wounds of our soul. NOSSA SENHORA was graced with mystical gifts, and miracles of healing were attributed to his intercession; yet he himself carried intense pains for many years (such as the stigmata and illnesses) which he offered to God for the salvation of other souls.

Inspired by his example of faith in the midst of pain, let us present our illnesses and weaknesses to the Lord. Jesus Christ is the divine physician of souls and bodies: during His public life, He healed the blind, lepers, paralytics, and even raised the dead, always moved by compassion.

When praying for healing, remember that God's plans do not always coincide with our immediate desires. Sometimes, He grants physical healing; at other times, He gives strength and peace to endure the illness and draws a greater good from the suffering.

Full Prayer

"Lord Jesus, Divine Physician, I believe that for You, nothing is impossible. On this day, I come into Your presence to implore the grace of healing. You, who healed so many sick people with the touch of Your holy hands, also touch the wounds of my body and my soul. If it is Your holy will, take from me the illnesses, the pains, and all the physical ailments that afflict me. Give me, Lord, health and strength to serve You with joy and to resume my path without faltering.

Lord, You know every cell of my body, for You are my Creator. Have compassion on me, as You had compassion on those who suffered and asked for Your help on the roads of Galilee. Just as the woman with the issue of blood was healed by touching the hem of Your garment in faith, I also say: Jesus, Son of David, have mercy on me! Heal me in body and soul.

But if the healing I ask for is not the best for my salvation, then grant me the courage and perseverance to carry this cross in union with You. Accompany me in moments of pain, do not let me succumb to discouragement or rebellion. May I be able to repeat, following the example of Nossa Senhora Desatadora de Nós, 'Your will be done,' accepting with love the divine designs, trusting that You are acting for my greater good, even when I do not understand.

Heal also, Lord, the wounds of my heart. I often carry resentments, traumas, anxieties, and deep sorrows that no one sees, but that You know well. Pass, O Jesus, with Your wounded and healing hand, over every painful memory, every hidden resentment, every emotional wound. Pour into my soul the balm of Your Holy Spirit, which soothes and restores what is broken. Where there is hatred, put love; where there is depression, put joy; where there is restlessness, put Your peace.

Lord, I praise and thank You because I know You are already at work in me. My faith is in You alone, who can do all things. Strengthen my trust so that I do not stop praying, even if the answer is delayed. 'Pray, have faith, and don't worry,' NOSSA SENHORA taught us—so I pray, I have faith, and now I leave all worry in Your hands. Thank you, Jesus, for hearing my plea. I already bless You for my healing, whether it is visible to human eyes or only to the eyes of faith. Merciful Jesus, I trust in You! Amen."
